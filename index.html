The Well of Wonder

The Well of Wonder is an exciting new series created for curious young minds. Each warm, beautifully illustrated book helps children explore a big question - from history and science to culture, creativity, and everyday life - through clear explanations and engaging stories.

Look for the first title through Ingram and Amazon beginning October 1.

A forthcoming Well of Wonder title

Where Did Christmas Come From?

Join Hazel and her father on Christmas Eve as they explore the stories and traditions behind Christmas - including the Nativity, Winter Solstice, Saturnalia, Yule, Christmas trees, wreaths, and more.

A cozy, illustrated journey for curious children ages 6-10.

About the Author

Emily Lovejoy

Emily Lovejoy has loved writing since early childhood. She worked as a cartoonist and animator early in her career and now enjoys writing and designing children's books with an educational flair.

Emily lives in Washington state with her two children, her dog, and her garden. She enjoys writing, studying history and science, and playing guitar.
